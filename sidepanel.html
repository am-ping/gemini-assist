How to Get Your Free Google API Key

Follow these steps to obtain your free Google API key for use with this extension:

  1. Open the Google AI Studio: Click here to visit the Google AI Studio.

  2. Log in to your Google account, if not already logged in.

  3. Once on the AI Studio page, locate and click the button labeled "Create API Key".

  4. Copy the API key after it is generated.

  5. Paste the key above and click on "Save Key" to store it.
